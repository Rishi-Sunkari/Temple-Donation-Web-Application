About Temple

Sri Govindharaja Swamy Devasthanam

A Place of Divine Blessings and Goodwill Nestled peacefully in Jagadgiri Gutta, Sri Govindharaja Swamy Devasthanam is a place of profound spirituality and compassion. Founded in 2008 by Sri Haripuri Narahari Swamy, the temple's creation was inspired by a vision from Lord Sri Lakshmi Narayana, guiding its purpose and significance.

At the heart of the temple lies Lord Govindha, a symbol of divine love and kindness. People from all walks of life are drawn to His presence, seeking solace and blessings in their lives. Known for removing obstacles and granting blessings, Lord Govindha holds a special place in the hearts of His devotees, guiding them through life's challenges with grace and compassion.

Beyond its religious significance, Sri Govindharaja Swamy Devasthanam is dedicated to serving the community. Regular events like annadanam, where free meals are provided to those in need, reflect the temple's commitment to compassion and generosity. Through such activities, the temple embodies the teachings of Lord Govindha, encouraging selfless service and kindness towards others.
